ROSE POINTE BASKETBALL LEAGUE PLAYER AWARDS CRITERIAS AND COMPUTATION

  1. Most Valuable Player (Elimination Round)
    2. CRITERIAS
    1. Minimum of 4 games played in the elimination round (total of 6 games).
    2. No “Unsportsmanlike conduct” commitment and no more than 2 Technical Fouls.
    3. MVP will not automatically come from the winningest team during elimination round.
    4. Player with the highest Total Statistical Points will win the MVP award. (TSP x % based on team standing).
    COMPUTATION
    1. Most Valuable Player to be determined by the Total basketball statistical points. (Points + Rebounds + Assists + Steals + Blocks) = Total Statistical Points.
    2. Additional percentage will be added to the Total Statistical Points of player based on their team standing after the elimination round.
    RANKED %
    1st 50%
    2nd 30%
    3rd 15%
    4th 5%

    How it works?

    PLAYER TSP TEAM RANKING % FINAL TSP
    Player A 830 1st 50% 830 + 415(50% of 830) = 1245
    Player B 700 2nd 30% 700 + 210(30% of 700) = 910
    Player C 1100 3rd 15% 1100 + 165(15% of 1100) = 1265 (MVP)
    Player D 900 4th 5% 900 + 45(5% of 900) = 945
  2. Mythical 5 Award
    3. CRITERIAS
    1. Minimum of 4 games played in the elimination round (total of 6 games).
    2. No “Unsportsmanlike conduct” commitment and no more than 2 Technical Fouls.
    3. Mythical 5 players may come from any of the 4 teams based on their Total Statistical Points.
    4. The top 5 players with the highest Total Statistical Points will be our Mythical 5 awardee.
    5. In case of tie between the No. 5 and No.6, team rank of player will be the tie breaker. If same team, it will be games played.
    COMPUTATION
    1. Mythical 5 players selection to be determined by the Total basketball statistical points. (Points + Rebounds + Assists + Steals + Blocks) = Total Statistical Points.
  3. Sixth Man of the League
    4. CRITERIAS
    1. Minimum of 4 games played in the elimination round (total of 6 games).
    2. No “Unsportsmanlike conduct” commitment and no more than 2 Technical Fouls.
    3. Sixth Man of the league should not be part of the first five for all the games played, meaning he always comes from the bench every game.

      (This will be monitored by the committees)

    4. Player with the highest Total Statistical Points will win the Sixth Man of the League.
    COMPUTATION
    1. Sixth Man of the league to be determined by the Total basketball statistical points. (Points + Rebounds + Assists + Steals + Blocks) = Total Statistical Points
  4. Most Defensive Player
    5. CRITERIAS
    1. Minimum of 4 games played in the elimination round (total of 6 games).
    2. No “Unsportsmanlike conduct” commitment and no more than 2 Technical Fouls.
    COMPUTATION
    1. Most Defensive Player to be determined by the Total defensive statistical points. (Rebounds + Steals + Blocks) = Total Defensive Statistical Points.
  5. Most Valuable Player (Finals MVP)
    6. CRITERIAS
    1. No “Unsportsmanlike conduct” commitment and no more than 2 Technical Fouls.
    2. MVP will automatically come from the Champion Team.
    3. Player with the highest Total Statistical Points during the Finals game will win the MVP award.
    COMPUTATION
    1. Most Valuable Player to be determined by the Total basketball statistical points. Finals Game only (Points + Rebounds + Assists + Steals + Blocks) = Total Statistical Points.
